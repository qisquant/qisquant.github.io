Today I woud like to show how to get FX data from [Sauder School of Business](http://fx.sauder.ubc.ca/data.html).



{% highlight r %}
#*****************************************************************
# Get FX rates
#*****************************************************************
out  = qis.fx.sauder.data(1995, 2018, 'USD', 'AUD,CAD,EUR,GBP,CHF') 

#*****************************************************************
# Plot FX rates
#*****************************************************************
qis.plot.xts(out,main='Exchange Rates')
{% endhighlight %}
